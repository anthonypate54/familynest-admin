FamilyNest Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 20, 2025

Welcome to FamilyNest. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe, secure experience for all our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using the FamilyNest application, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by the terms described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Create an Account: When you register, we collect your username, email address, and password.
  • Create a Profile: Information you add to your profile such as display name, profile picture, and family relationships.
  • Send Messages: Content of messages, photos, videos, and other communications you send through the app.
  • Family Management: Information about family members you add or invite to the app.
  • Subscription Information: When you purchase a subscription, we collect payment information, which is processed by our payment providers (Google Play or Apple App Store).

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you use our app, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Information: How you use the app, features you access, and time spent on the app.
  • Log Information: App errors, crash reports, and performance data.

1.3 Permissions We Request

Our app may request the following permissions, which you can grant or deny:

  • Camera: To take photos and videos to share with family members.
  • Photo Library: To access existing photos and videos to share with family members.
  • Notifications: To send you important updates and message notifications.
  • Storage: To save media files shared within the app.

We only request permissions that are necessary for core app functionality, and we explain why each permission is needed when we request it.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the FamilyNest application
  • Process and manage your account and subscription
  • Facilitate communication between family members
  • Send you technical notices, updates, and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Monitor and analyze trends and usage to improve the app experience
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities
  • Protect the rights and property of FamilyNest and others

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Family Members: Content you share within the app is visible to the family members you've connected with.
  • Service Providers: We work with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, analytics, and customer service.
  • Payment Processors: We share information with payment processors (Google Play and Apple App Store) to process subscription payments.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, legal process, or government request.
  • Business Transfers: If FamilyNest is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Subscription Management

FamilyNest offers subscription services processed through Google Play (for Android users) or Apple App Store (for iOS users). Please note:

  • Subscription payments are processed by Google Play or Apple App Store, not directly by FamilyNest.
  • To manage, change, or cancel your subscription, please use the subscription management tools provided by Google Play or Apple App Store.
  • When you cancel a subscription, you will continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.
  • Our app may store your subscription status to provide appropriate access to premium features.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

FamilyNest is designed for family use, including by children with parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13 without proper consent, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove that information.

7. Your Choices

You have several choices regarding your information:

  • Account Information: You can update your account information through the app settings.
  • Permissions: You can change app permissions through your device settings.
  • Communications: You can opt out of promotional communications by following the instructions in those messages.
  • Delete Account: You can request to delete your account by contacting our support team.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. We will delete or anonymize your information upon request or when it is no longer needed for the purposes collected, unless retention is necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, and processed in, countries other than the country in which you reside. These countries may have different data protection laws than your country. By using FamilyNest, you consent to this transfer, storing, and processing.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
